About the Author

The brilliant mind behind this project is a 14 year old from Quezon City

Emman Gabriel Ella is student from Philippine Science High School-Central Luzon Campus

He organized this project with the goal of relaxation and detoxing from school activities

He is very fond of sweets and pastries, so he thought of a way to express these interests through the media

The website is all about the things Emman like, such as pastries, frappuccinos, sweet food, etc.

The author aims to share these interests with other people so they can also explore the delight of trying these food

This student intents to have these delicacies be seen online as a way of destressing after a long day of work

Seeing these food and drinks will keep your motor running, and will make your body crave for it as a way of rewarding yourself and relaxation

This is also a way of advertising these different type of food that might not get much recognition for its delicious taste
