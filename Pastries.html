Ensaymada

It is a type of soft, sweet filipino pastry covered with butter and sugar, and has a lot of grated cheese on top. It was adapted from the Spanish but has evolved into a dessert loved by Filipinos.

Baklava

A layered pastry Turkish dessert made of filo dough, filled with crushed nuts, and sweetened with honey syrup.

Jamaican Patty

The Jamaican patty is a semicircular pastry that is filled with various fillings such as beef, lamb, vegetables, etc. on the inside and has a flaky shell on the outside.

Rurki

This sweet delicacy is a type of cream roll that consists of a thin pastry that's usually filled with whipped cream or custard filling. Very airy and light desserts that can be made out of a flaky or puff pastry.

Beignet

A deep-fried French pastry, that has an eggy batter that puffs and creates an airy inside, then topped of with powedered sugar.

Egg Tart

A classic delicious pastry with a flaky outer shell with a creamy, firm egg custard in the center. It has a rich cream, with a strong egg flavor.
